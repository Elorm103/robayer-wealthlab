Overview

What this service covers

Business Financial Advisory is practical, educational guidance for people running a small business or early-stage venture — help understanding cash flow, separating personal and business money, and building simple habits that support growth. It's a complement to formal accounting, tax, and audit services, not a replacement for them.

Is this for you?

Who this service is for

  • You're running a small business and personal and business money are mixed together
  • You're not sure how to read your own cash flow
  • You're planning to grow and want a practical financial footing first
  • You want plain guidance, not a formal audit or compliance service
What you'll learn

By the end of this engagement

  • How to separate personal and business finances properly
  • How to understand and plan around your business's cash flow
  • What financial habits actually support growth, not just survival
  • Simple record-keeping habits that make future decisions easier

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this the same as hiring an accountant?

No — this is complementary financial education and advisory, not a replacement for licensed accounting, tax, or audit services. Many readers use both.

Can you help with a business plan?

The financial side of a business plan — cash flow, basic projections, financial habits — is within scope; broader business strategy is not the focus of this service.

Do you work with registered and unregistered businesses?

Yes — the fundamentals covered apply at any stage, including very early or informal ventures.

Is this only for businesses based in Accra?

No — sessions can be arranged remotely; location isn't a barrier.

What if my business has irregular income?

That's a common reality for small businesses in Ghana, and it's factored directly into the guidance rather than treated as an edge case.

How technical do I need to be?

Not technical at all — this is explained in plain language, with no assumption of accounting or finance background.

Do you provide legal or tax advice?

No — this service is financial education and advisory only. For legal or tax matters, we'd point you toward a licensed professional.
