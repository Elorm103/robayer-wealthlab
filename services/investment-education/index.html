Overview

What this service covers

Investment Education walks you through the investment vehicles actually available to Ghanaians — treasury bills, the Ghana Stock Exchange, mobile-money-based savings products, and how they compare — in plain language, with no jargon and no pressure. The goal is understanding, not a recommendation: you leave able to make your own informed decision, or with a clearer sense of what to ask a licensed investment professional if you choose to go further.

Is this for you?

Who this service is for

  • You've saved something and don't know what a sensible next step looks like
  • You've never bought a treasury bill or touched the GSE before
  • You want to understand mobile-money savings products before committing to one
  • You want to understand your options, not be told what to buy
What you'll learn

By the end of this session

  • How treasury bills work, and what buying one actually involves
  • The basics of the Ghana Stock Exchange, explained without jargon
  • How mobile-money savings products compare to more formal investment options
  • How to think about risk before you put money anywhere

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this regulated investment advice?

No. Robayer WealthLab is an educational and coaching platform, not a licensed investment advisor. This service helps you understand your options so you can decide for yourself, or consult a licensed professional for specific recommendations.

What if I've never bought a treasury bill before?

That's exactly who this service is built for — no prior experience assumed.

Do you recommend specific stocks or products?

No. We explain how things work and what to consider — the decision of what, if anything, to buy is always yours.

Is mobile money savings a legitimate way to build wealth?

It can be a useful savings habit-builder — this session covers honestly where it fits alongside other options, including its limits.

Can businesses use this service too?

Yes — the fundamentals covered apply whether you're investing personal savings or a business's surplus, though for business-specific planning, see Business Financial Advisory.

What happens if I want to go further after this?

You're welcome to book a follow-up, or move on to Personal Financial Coaching for ongoing support as your investing habits develop.
