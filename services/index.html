Questions

Frequently asked questions

How do I choose which service is right for me?

If you're not sure, book a consultation and describe your situation — we'll help you figure out the right starting point.

Do you offer one-off sessions or ongoing support?

Both. Financial Education and Investment Education are typically single focused sessions; Personal Financial Coaching is an ongoing relationship; Business Financial Advisory and Financial Literacy Workshops are scoped to your specific situation.

Is this only for individuals?

No. Financial Literacy Workshops and Business Financial Advisory are built for organizations, teams, and small businesses as well as individuals.

Do you provide licensed financial, investment, tax, or legal advice?

No. Robayer WealthLab is an educational and coaching platform. For licensed investment, tax, or legal advice, we'd point you toward the appropriate licensed professional.

What happens after I request a consultation?

We normally reply within 2–3 business days to confirm details and next steps — sometimes a little longer during a busy stretch.

How much do services cost?

Pricing varies by service and is shared directly when you reach out.

Can I request something that isn't listed here?

Yes — reach out through the Contact page and describe what you're looking for. Reader requests are exactly how new services get shaped.
