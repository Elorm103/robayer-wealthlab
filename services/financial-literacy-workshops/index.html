Overview

What this service covers

Financial Literacy Workshops bring the same plain-language, no-hype approach to a group setting, whether that's an employer wanting to support staff, a community or alumni group, or a school. Workshops are built around the group's context, typically covering budgeting fundamentals, saving habits, and an introduction to the investment options available to Ghanaians.

Is this for you?

Who this service is for

  • You're an employer wanting to offer real financial-literacy value to your team
  • You're organizing a community, church, or alumni group session
  • You represent a school or student group wanting a practical session
  • You want a shared, group learning experience rather than individual sessions
What you'll learn

What a workshop typically covers

  • Budgeting fundamentals appropriate to the group's context
  • Practical saving habits, including mobile-money-based approaches
  • A plain-language introduction to Ghanaian investment options
  • Whatever custom topic is agreed for your specific group

Questions

Frequently asked questions

What size groups do you work with?

This varies. Share your group's size when you reach out, and we'll confirm whether the format fits.

Can workshops be customized to our group's needs?

Yes, the topic and format are agreed together rather than delivered as a fixed, one-size-fits-all session.

Are workshops in-person or virtual?

Either, depending on what works best for your group.

Is there a minimum group size?

This is discussed directly when you reach out, based on your group's context.

Can attendees get individual follow-up afterward?

Yes, attendees are welcome to explore Personal Financial Coaching or book their own consultation afterward.

How do we book a workshop for our organization?

Book a consultation through the Contact page, and share your group's size and context so we can confirm a fit.

Do workshops include investment recommendations?

No, workshops are educational, covering how things work rather than recommending specific products or investments.
