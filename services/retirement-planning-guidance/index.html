Overview

What this service covers

Retirement Planning Guidance helps you understand what retirement realistically looks like in the Ghanaian context — how SSNIT and pension contributions work, where a typical pension income falls short of typical living costs, and how personal savings and investing can help close that gap. Whether retirement is decades away or close, the goal is a realistic, honest picture rather than guesswork.

Is this for you?

Who this service is for

  • You're mid-career and haven't thought seriously about retirement yet
  • You're approaching retirement and want a realistic picture of your income
  • You don't fully understand how your SSNIT or pension contributions work
  • You want to know how personal savings fit alongside a pension
What you'll learn

By the end of this session

  • How SSNIT and pension contributions actually work
  • How to think honestly about the gap between pension income and real living costs
  • How personal savings and investing can help close that gap
  • How to think about timeline and risk as retirement gets closer

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this connected to SSNIT directly?

No — this is independent, educational guidance about how SSNIT and pensions generally work, not a SSNIT service or affiliated program.

Will you manage my pension for me?

No — this service is educational guidance, not pension management or licensed investment advice.

What if retirement is still far away for me?

It's still valuable — starting earlier generally makes the eventual plan easier, not harder.

Can retirees or people near retirement use this too?

Yes — the guidance adjusts to whatever stage you're actually at.

Does this replace professional pension or legal advice?

No — for formal pension administration or legal matters, we'd point you toward the appropriate licensed professional or SSNIT directly.

How is this different from Investment Education?

This service is framed specifically around retirement timeline and income planning; Investment Education covers investment options more broadly.

Do you guarantee a specific retirement outcome?

No — this service provides education and realistic planning guidance, not guarantees about future income or returns.
