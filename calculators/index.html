Are these calculators free?

Yes — every calculator here is free, with no login or sign-up required.

No. They're educational projection tools based on the numbers you enter, not recommendations to buy any specific product.

No. Every calculation runs entirely in your browser — nothing you enter is saved or transmitted anywhere.
