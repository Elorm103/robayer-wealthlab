Is this for you?

Who should book a consultation

  • You've read about a topic and want to go deeper for your specific situation
  • You're ready to talk through a decision with someone directly, not just read about it
  • You want ongoing coaching or a single, focused session — either way, this is the starting point
  • You represent a business or organization looking for advisory or a workshop

Before you request

What's helpful to have ready

  • A rough sense of your current income, expenses, or savings, if relevant to your question
  • Any specific numbers connected to your goal — an amount, a timeline, a rate you're considering
  • The questions you actually want answered — there's no such thing as a silly one
  • Nothing you're not comfortable sharing — bring only what helps the conversation
Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is a consultation request the same as booking an appointment?

No. There is no automatic booking or live calendar yet — every request is reviewed personally, and we confirm a time together afterward.

How quickly will I hear back?

Normally within 2–3 business days — sometimes a little longer during a busy stretch.

How much does a consultation cost?

It depends on the type and length of consultation — we'll confirm this with you when we respond to your request.

What if my situation spans more than one category?

Choose whichever category feels closest, and mention the rest in your description — we'll take it from there.

What if I'm not in Ghana?

That's fine — consultations can be arranged remotely regardless of where you're based.

Is my information kept private?

Yes — see our Privacy Policy for details on how information you share is handled.

What if I don't hear back?

Check your spam folder first, then feel free to email us directly — occasionally requests need a second look.

Will I receive licensed financial advice during a consultation?

No. Consultations are educational and advisory — not licensed investment, legal, tax, or accounting advice.

Robayer WealthLab provides financial education and coaching — not licensed investment, legal, tax, or accounting advice. A consultation is educational and advisory in nature, not a substitute for professional licensed services where those are required.
