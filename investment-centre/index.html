Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is the Investment Centre financial advice?

No. Every topic page is educational — it explains how something works, its benefits and risks, and who it typically suits. It doesn't recommend specific products, companies, or stocks.

Which topic should I start with?

If you're new to investing, Emergency Funds and Treasury Bills are common starting points — or try the Beginner Investor learning path above.

Is this a news site?

No. The Investment Centre doesn't publish market news or commentary — it's a fixed set of educational topic pages, kept accurate and revisited as needed rather than updated daily.

How is a Learning Path different from a topic page?

A topic page covers one subject in depth. A Learning Path strings several topics, calculators, services, and goals together in a suggested order for a broader objective, like becoming a beginner investor.

Will more topics be added?

The Investment Centre is built to grow — new topics can be added using the same structure without changing how existing pages work.

Where do I go for guidance on my specific situation?

Request a consultation — a conversation can go further than any educational page by working through your actual numbers and goals.
