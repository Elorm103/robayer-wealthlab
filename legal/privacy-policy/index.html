This policy explains what information Robayer WealthLab collects, why, and what choices you have about it. We've tried to write it in the same plain language as everything else on this site.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you give us directly — for example, your name and email address, when you email us or subscribe to the newsletter.
  • Newsletter subscription details — your email address, and whether you're currently subscribed.
  • Purchase information — if you buy an eBook or other digital product, we receive your email address (as confirmed by our payment processor, Paystack), which product you bought, the amount paid, and a purchase reference. This is used to verify your payment, grant you access to your purchase, and send your receipt and download instructions.
  • Analytics data, such as pages visited and general location, if and when an analytics tool is enabled on this site.
  • Device and browser information — such as browser type and screen size — collected automatically by any website you visit.

We never see or store your card number, mobile money PIN, or other payment details — those are entered directly on Paystack's own secure checkout page, never on our systems.

How we use information

  • To deliver the newsletter you've subscribed to.
  • To verify a payment, grant access to a digital product you've purchased, and send your receipt and download instructions.
  • To understand how the website is used, so we can improve it.
  • To respond to your questions and provide support when you contact us.
  • To let you know about new guides, resources, or product updates.

Third-party services

We use trusted third-party services to run parts of this site, including:

  • Paystack — our checkout provider, for processing eBook and digital product purchases. Your payment details are handled entirely by Paystack; we never see or store them.
  • Resend — our email service provider, for delivering the newsletter, purchase receipts, download instructions, and replies to messages you send us.
  • An analytics tool, such as Google Analytics, for understanding site usage — not yet active.

If we turn on analytics or add another third-party service in future, we'll update this policy and let subscribers know.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us, including limiting who has access to it and using reputable service providers.

No method of storing or transmitting data online is completely secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security — but we're committed to handling your information responsibly.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • See a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correct or update your information.
  • Delete your information.
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter — using the link in every email, or by contacting us directly.

Contact

Questions about this policy, or want to exercise any of the rights above? Reach us at hello@robayerwealthlab.com, or visit the Contact page for other ways to get in touch.
