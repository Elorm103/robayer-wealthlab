      Questions

      Frequently asked questions

      Is the Goal Planner powered by artificial intelligence?

      No. It's a structured recommendation engine: your answers are mapped to a fixed set of recommendations defined ahead of time for each goal, not generated by a language model or AI.

      Is this financial advice?

      No. It's educational: a starting point for your own research and, if useful, a conversation during a consultation. It isn't personalized advice from a licensed professional.

      Is my information saved or sent anywhere?

      No. Every calculation runs entirely in your browser, and nothing you enter is saved or transmitted anywhere.

      How are the recommendations chosen?

      Each goal has a fixed set of relevant calculators, services, and resources defined in advance. Your answers drive the suggested monthly savings figure; they don't change which calculators or services are recommended.

      Can I plan for a goal that isn't listed?

      Not yet. This first version covers 8 common goals. You can also use the standalone calculators directly, or reach out through the Contact page to suggest a goal type.

      What if I don't know my exact numbers?

      A rough estimate is fine, and you can come back and adjust your answers any time to see how the numbers change.

      Does the suggested savings figure account for inflation?

      Not automatically. If you want an inflation-adjusted view, try the Investment Growth calculator, which has an inflation toggle built in.

      What should I do after getting a recommendation?

      Explore the calculator(s) and service(s) it points you to, or request a consultation for guidance built around your exact situation.
